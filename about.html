My story

I'm Siyi Gao! I'm currently a junior-year student in NanoEngineering at UCSD. I'm from Chengdu, a city in south China, where is the birthplace of hotpot. People in Chengdu play magic with all kinds of peppers, especially the spicy ones. For this reason, I found it's pretty hard to find the truly spicy and tasty restaurant in America. Personally, I feel pepper could reveal part of my life philosophy. Since there are plenty of pepper in the world, it could be spice in a course, the main ingredients, or just a raw pepper. The destiny of pepper are closely related to the people who pick it up and put it in the pan. The pepper can dance with extraordinary imaginations or lay on the plate with dead silence. Don't worry, if you got fuzzled by my rhetoric! I mean pepper is just like the life, it will surprize if you treat it with passion and imagination, but it can also put you down easily, if you care nothing about it. I would like to pursue a career combining Engineering with design.

Life is a about working with what you love!
